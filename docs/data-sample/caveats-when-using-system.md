# Caveats
A lot of the front-end interactions do not offer indication of success even if the operations have been completed successfully.

The list outlines these caveats and ways to verify the operations
- Registration
  - No indication will be provided.
  - Simply login with your new credentials
- Add seller to seller group
  - No indication for success
  - Login into the seller's account, the seller should see all the listings by the seller group.
- Removing seller from seller group
  - No indication for success
  - Login to the seller's account, the seller should have no listings under his name.
